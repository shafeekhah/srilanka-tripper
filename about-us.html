About sri lanka tripper

Hand-crafted Travel Experiences

Sri Lanka Tripper is a Sri Lankan owned company operating also in Sri Lanka has many years of experience in the Travel & Tourism Industry. Our teams of professionals are dedicated to provide outstanding and reliable customer service by helping our visitors to discover and enjoy the beauty in Sri Lanka.

Sri Lanka Tripper offers affordable travels and tours packages including all the breathtaking views in Sri Lanka. We are happy to walk with you through rural outbacks, scenic mountains, valleys, ancient cultural villages, tea estates, beautiful golden sandy beaches and feeling the real taste of Ceylon spices with the delicious rice & curry and enjoy a warm cup of Pure Ceylon Tea; the best way to experience authentic Sri Lanka.

Sri Lanka is a country that has great cultural diversity from its people from different ethnicities, land rich with spirit and culture, nature, adventure, wellness, and tremendous wildlife. Be adventurous and explore the country through a walking tour, cycling tour, seat in coach tour, private vehicle, Air taxi or an attractive train ride to the hill country (Kandy to Ella), through foggy tea estates and old pine forests which is known as one of the most scenic train rides in the world.

Feel right at home among the welcoming warmth of our people. We; Sri Lanka Tripper, the emerging tour company in this small island, hand crafted tours offers you the chance to travel in Sri Lanka and satisfy your valuable travel dreams!
